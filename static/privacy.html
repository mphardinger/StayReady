Stay Ready — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Stay Ready is a meal planning app for households. This page explains what information the app stores and what happens to it. The short version: your data exists so the app works, it is never sold, and you can delete it yourself at any time.

What we collect

When you create an account we store a username, a display name, and a password (stored only as a salted hash, never as the password itself). We do not ask for or collect your email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, or photos.

As you use the app, we store the content your household creates: recipes, planned meals, pantry items, shopping and sale lists, grocery budgets, and expense records including which household member paid. This content is shared with the other accounts in your household, because that is the point of the app.

The app uses one essential session cookie to keep you signed in. There are no advertising, analytics, or tracking cookies, and no third-party trackers of any kind.

How your data is used

Only to run the app for you and your household. We do not sell your data, share it with third parties, use it for advertising, or use it to train AI models.

Where it lives

Data is stored on PythonAnywhere hosting and all traffic is encrypted with HTTPS. Login attempts are rate-limited to protect your account.

Nutrition information

Nutrition values and diet tags shown in the app are estimates, not laboratory measurements, and are not medical advice. If you follow a renal, low-FODMAP, diabetic, or any other medical diet, verify amounts with your care team before relying on them.

Deleting your account and data

You can delete your account from inside the app at any time: open the House tab, find the Account card, and choose Delete account. You will be asked for your password to confirm. This works the same in the Android app and at stayready.pythonanywhere.com, which is the same application.

Deletion is permanent. If yours is the last account in the household, every recipe, planned meal, pantry item, and expense record in that household is erased with it. If other accounts remain, the shared household content stays with them, including expense history that names household members.

If you cannot sign in to delete your account, email the address below and we will delete it for you.

Children

Stay Ready is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.
